Droidparts

is a carefully crafted Android library that includes:

  • dependency injection
  • SQLite object-relational mapping
  • JSON (de)serialization
  • AsyncTasks with Exceptions, progress & result reporting
  • improved logger (log any object, no need for tag & save characters by typing L. instead of Log.)
  • RESTClient for GETting, PUTting, POSTing, DELETing & InputStream-getting. Also speaks JSON
  • numerous Utils

It also fully supports Fragments in cooperation with ActionBarSherlock.
